<a href="https://github.com/asder8215/Arduino-Fortune-Wheel-Game" target="_blank"><b>Name the Game, Spin the Wheel</b></a>
<em>February 2022 - May 2022</em>
&bull; Group project featuring an user-centric game allowing players to imagine a game (e.g. 21, HighLow, etc.) to play on a
spinning wheel numbered 1-8 and press on arcade buttons to start or halt the wheel
&bull; Designed and implemented the circuitry for the arcade buttons to the Arduino board and programmed button presses to
initiate and stop the motor
