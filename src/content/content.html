    Fun facts:
    • This is my 3rd attempt at developing a personal website for myself. The past two times I tried making my own website –once during the summer between junior and senior year of high school in mostly HTML & CSS and a little bit of JS and the other time during freshman year of college in HTML/CSS/JS & React framework– it was a bust; I didn't like how it ended up being.

Hence, I started my development of this website.
    • This website was initially only supposed to have a terminal on the right half and the content box on the left half, but after receiving feedback from some friends, I decided to add in a file explorer for easier navigation.
